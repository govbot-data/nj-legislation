STATEMENT This bill prohibits banning and restricting access to books in public libraries. The bill prohibits a governing body of any free public library, joint free public library, free county library, and regional library from banning or restricting access to books or other educational resource materials, except as may be required by federal law. The billВ’s provisions should not be construed to require a free public library to purchase, or otherwise acquire, a book or resource for inclusion in its collection.
